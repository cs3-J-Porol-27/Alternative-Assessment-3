Jor's Recommended Films!

Our Beloved Summer (2021)

Rated: 5/5

Marked: Must Watch

About: A romantic comedy centered on Choi Ung (Choi Woo-shik) and Kook Yeon-soo (Kim Da-mi), who separated with a pact to never cross paths again during their youth. However, fate takes an unexpected turm when a documentary they made in high school a decade ago becomes a viral sensation. Their producer friend compels them to reunite on camera, leading to the portrayal of their intricate emotions and personal development in the series.

Comments: This kdrama depicted love and grief so well. The main character's have great chemistry, everyone did a great job in showing what their characters truly convey and you can just feel the emotions through the screen.

Little Women (2019)

Rated: 5/5

Marked: Must Watch

About: The story takes place during and after the Civil War and unfolds the tale of the March family, with a primary focus on the four sisters: Meg, Jo, Beth, and Amy. These young girls face challenges and develop as a family while their father is away serving in the war, gaining an understanding of the importance of diligence, selflessness, and affection.

Comments: Genuinely such a wholesome movie that depicts the life of a girl facing life events and realizing the importance of girlhood. This movie presents a lot of moral lessons that we can all apply in our daily life.
